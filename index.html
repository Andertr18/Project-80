Welcome to Project 80

Mission Statement: Helping pitchers and catchers succeed on and off the field.

Objective: Allow athletes to put themselves in a position to be successful and reach their goals.

What is Project 80? Project 80 is a training organization designed to help pitchers and catchers get to 80 mph and progress from there. The concept is simple: 80 mph is where college coaches begin showing interest. Once there, every MPH gain is a celebration, continuing the pursuit of the athlete’s goals.

What We Offer

Pitching

  • Velocity Training
  • Arm Care Regimens
  • Throwing Programs (In and Out of Season)
  • Weight Programs (Specific to Each Athlete)
  • Pitch and Pitch Type Development
  • Pitch Executing Trainings
  • Mechanics Training

Catchers

  • One and Two Knee Form Catching
  • Velocity Training
  • Framing and Technique
  • Pitch Calling and Understanding
  • Blocking
  • Ability to Work with Live Pitchers
